From Kitchen Waste to Rich Vermicompost & Vermiwash

System Overview

Complete Two-Stage System Components:
  • Two 20-L Bokashi buckets - rotational fermentation
  • Milk crate - neutralizing pre-compost
  • Two 60-L vermicompost drums - continuous processing
  • Collection containers - leachate and vermiwash

Why This System Works

The combination of Bokashi fermentation followed by vermicomposting creates a synergistic effect that preserves nutrients while producing stable, nutrient-rich humus.
